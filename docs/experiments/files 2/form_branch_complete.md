## Complete Diagnostic List

### Proportion Issues (3)
1. **diag_form_proportion_opening** - Opening section too long
   - OMT: Formal Sections in General
   - Solutions: Compression, delay development, front-load material, strategic cuts

2. **diag_form_proportion_development** - Development too short
   - OMT: Sonata Form
   - Solutions: Extend contrast, fragmentation, modulation, sequential treatment

3. **diag_form_proportion_ending** - Ending feels rushed
   - OMT: Formal Sections in General
   - Solutions: Extend cadential space, coda addition, plagal extension, tonic prolongation

### Transition Issues (3)
4. **diag_form_transitions_abrupt** - Abrupt section changes
   - OMT: Formal Sections in General
   - Solutions: Transitional passage, preparation, retransition, elision with preparation

5. **diag_form_transitions_overlap** - Too much overlap/elision
   - OMT: Harmonic Elision
   - Solutions: Clear cadence, breathing space, articulation, reduce overlap

6. **diag_form_transitions_key** - Key change feels unmotivated
   - OMT: Extended Tonicization and Modulation
   - Solutions: Pivot preparation, sequential transition, chromatic voice leading, enharmonic reinterpretation

### Boundary Issues (3)
7. **diag_form_boundaries_cadences** - Cadences are weak or absent
   - OMT: Strengthening Endings with V7
   - Solutions: PAC strengthening, dominant preparation, cadential weight, avoid deceptive cadences

8. **diag_form_boundaries_phrases** - Phrase lengths obscure structure
   - OMT: Phrase Archetypes
   - Solutions: Regular phrase lengths, clear antecedent-consequent, metric clarity, hypermeter

9. **diag_form_boundaries_markers** - No textural or thematic boundary markers
   - OMT: Texture
   - Solutions: Textural contrast, thematic return, register shift, orchestration change

### Predictability Issues (2)
10. **diag_form_predictable_conventional** - Too conventional
    - OMT: Phrase Archetypes
    - Solutions: Hybrid forms, formal innovation, truncation, expansion

11. **diag_form_predictable_surprises** - No surprises or deviations
    - OMT: Phrase Archetypes
    - Solutions: False recapitulation, extended development, formal subversion, unexpected modulation

### Return/Recapitulation Issues (3)
12. **diag_form_return_literal** - Recapitulation too literal
    - OMT: Sonata Form
    - Solutions: Varied recapitulation, reharmonization, compression, embellishment

13. **diag_form_return_resolution** - Return doesn't feel like resolution
    - OMT: Sonata Form
    - Solutions: Dominant prolongation, retransition buildup, textural preparation, rhythmic intensification

14. **diag_form_return_completion** - No sense of completion
    - OMT: Formal Sections in General
    - Solutions: Coda addition, plagal extension, tonic prolongation, textural finality
